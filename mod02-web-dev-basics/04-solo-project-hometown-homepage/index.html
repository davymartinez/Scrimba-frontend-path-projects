Come to Buenos Aires

A city full of rich history, vibrant multiculturalism, and plenty of green spaces.

Top three activities to do in Buenos Aires

Enjoy the green outdoor areas

Visit Los Bosques de Palermo (Palermo's Forests), the city's largest green space where you can practice sports, walk around or just relax outdoors.

Revel in the cultural experience

Enjoy the many options in museums and theaters, such as the magnificent Teatro Colón, one the world's most important opera halls.

Eat out to your heart's content

Indulge your foodie side in places like El Patio de los Lecheros (The Milkmen's Yard), a culinary courtyard with plenty of tasty options.
